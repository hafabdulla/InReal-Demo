# Website & Landing Page Copy Reference

Use this template for all website copy: landing pages, homepage sections, property listings, conversion pages, etc.

## The InReal Web Register

**Tone:** Assured, editorial, like the homepage of a premium fintech.
**Purpose:** Convert and educate, not entertain.
**Principle:** Every word earns its place.

---

## Hero Section Template (Homepage / Landing Page)

**Components:**
1. **Headline** (8-12 words)
2. **Subheading** (15-25 words, explains the headline)
3. **Body copy** (2-3 short paragraphs, 40-60 words each)
4. **Primary CTA** (4-5 words)
5. **Secondary CTA** (optional, for lower intent)

**Example (Real Estate Access):**

```
HEADLINE:
"Real estate investing, starting at $500."

SUBHEADING:
"Own fractional shares of institutional-grade properties.
No $300k down payment. No landlord headaches."

BODY:
"Real estate built wealth for centuries.
But you needed $300,000, a lawyer, and time to manage it.

That's changed.

InReal lets you own property in Bangkok, Dubai, and Singapore for $500.
We handle the tenants, maintenance, and distributions.
You earn rental income."

PRIMARY CTA:
"Make your first investment"

SECONDARY CTA:
"Explore available properties"
```

## Feature Block Template

**When you have 3+ features to highlight:**

```
SECTION HEADLINE:
[Benefit-forward. Lead with outcome, not feature.]
"What makes InReal different" 
— BAD, because it's about us

"Why $500 investors now earn rental income"
— GOOD, because it's about them
```

**Per-feature structure:**
- **Icon/visual**
- **Feature name** (2-3 words)
- **Explanation** (1-2 sentences, 30-40 words)

**Example:**

| Feature | Copy |
|---------|------|
| 🏢 **Institutional Properties** | "Same properties that family offices and developers invest in. Just in smaller pieces. Fully tenanted, professionally managed, real returns." |
| ⏱️ **2-Minute Setup** | "No paperwork. No legal fees. Create an account, verify your identity, invest. Most investors start their first property in under 5 minutes." |
| 💰 **Low Entry Point** | "Fractional ownership means you set your amount. Own $500 or $5,000 worth — same institutional-grade property, same rental income stream." |
| 🌍 **Global Markets** | "Diversify across Bangkok, Dubai, Singapore. Real estate exposure without moving or managing. One app, multiple markets, one currency dashboard." |

## Pricing / Product Page Template

**Structure:**
1. **Section headline** (benefit-forward)
2. **Context paragraph** (40-50 words, explain why this matters)
3. **Comparison table OR numbered list** (features + InReal positioning)
4. **Objection handler** (FAQ section, 2-3 questions)
5. **CTA** (action-specific)

**Example: Fee Transparency**

```
HEADLINE:
"No surprises. Ever."

CONTEXT:
"Real estate investing shouldn't come with hidden fees,
surprise deductions, or complex calculations.
Here's exactly what you pay."

FEES TABLE:
| Charge | Amount | When? |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Management Fee | 1.5% annual | Taken quarterly from distributions |
| Acquisition Fee | 2% | One-time, when you invest |
| No exit fees | $0 | You can sell anytime via secondary market |
| No hidden charges | $0 | Everything is published and transparent |

COMPARISON:
InReal: 1.5% annual management fee
Direct ownership: 5-15% landlord time + 10-30% vacancy loss
REITs: 0.4-1.2% fee + surprise capital calls + illiquid
```

## Property Listing Copy Template

**Never write generic property descriptions.** Specificity is your competitive advantage.

**Structure:**
1. **Opening line** (1 sentence, most compelling fact)
2. **The property** (2-3 sentences: location, type, condition)
3. **The returns** (specific yield, occupancy, tenant quality)
4. **The experience** (what does owning this feel like?)
5. **Next step** (specific CTA)

**Example:**

```
HEADLINE:
"Bangkok Marina Residence — 2-Bed Waterfront"

OPENING:
"Fully tenanted apartment, 7.8% projected annual yield.
Expat-heavy neighborhood. High-income tenants. 96% occupancy history."

THE PROPERTY:
"2 bedrooms, 1.5 baths, 92 sqm.
River-facing views. Gym, pool, 24/7 security.
5 years old, professionally maintained."

THE RETURNS:
"Projected yield: 7.8% annually
Current occupancy: 100% (tenant through June 2027)
Average tenant tenure: 3.2 years
Neighborhood growth: +12% YoY appreciation (2019-2024)"

THE EXPERIENCE:
"Own this without managing it.
Tenants pay rent. We collect.
You earn distributions quarterly.
No vacancy risk — we handle tenant replacement."

INVESTMENT BREAKDOWN:
| Property Value | $125,000 |
| Your Investment | $500–$2,500 |
| Monthly Rental Income | ~$650/month (if you owned the whole property) |
| Your Share (at $500) | ~$2.60/month (plus appreciation) |
| Projected Annual Yield | 7.8% |

CTA:
"Invest in this property"
```

## FAQ / Objection Handler Template

**Place these near CTAs. Answer the most common objection first.**

```
Q: "What if I need my money back?"
A: "You can sell your shares anytime via our secondary market.
Liquidity depends on buyer demand — typically 1-4 weeks.
We're not a traditional REIT; this isn't day-trading, but it's faster than selling a real property."

Q: "How do I know the property is real?"
A: "Every property has a property profile with:
- Professional photos (2024 taken)
- Public deed/title number (searchable in local records)
- Third-party valuation (independent appraiser)
- Tenant agreement summary (anonymized)
- Property management reports (monthly updates)"

Q: "What if the tenant doesn't pay rent?"
A: "Our property managers handle tenant screening (top 20% credit scores).
If default occurs, we initiate eviction (legal fees covered).
In 10+ years, default rate: <0.3% across all portfolios.
Capital at risk. Past performance ≠ future results."
```

## Length Guidelines by Page Type

| Page Type | Ideal Word Count | Why |
|-----------|------------------|-----|
| Homepage hero | 150-200 | Quick, punchy, scannable |
| Feature section | 80-120 per feature | Each feature is independent |
| Pricing page | 300-500 total | People need clarity on costs |
| Property listing | 200-300 | Specific enough to convert, not overwhelming |
| About page | 400-600 | Allows narrative, builds trust |
| FAQ | 50-80 per answer | People are skimming for their question |
| Legal/Risk | No length limit | Compliance matters more than brevity |

## Copy Editing Checklist for Web Pages

Before publishing:

- [ ] **Headline passes the newspaper test:** Would this be a compelling headline in a business newspaper?
- [ ] **Subheading explains the headline:** Someone who only reads the headline + subheading understands what the product does.
- [ ] **Every CTA is unique:** No two CTAs on the same page say the same thing.
- [ ] **Numbers are cited:** Any claim ("fast," "easy," "trusted") is backed by a number or removed.
- [ ] **Passive voice is killed:** Sentences start with the actor, not the action.
- [ ] **Specificity test passed:** No sentence has zero numbers, proper nouns, or dates.
- [ ] **Risk disclosure is present:** Any mention of returns includes risk language.
- [ ] **Mobile readability:** Paragraphs are max 3 lines on mobile. Headings are short enough to fit.
- [ ] **Links are labeled well:** Someone knows where a link goes before clicking it (not "click here" or "learn more").

## Common Web Copy Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Example | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Vague benefit | "Flexible real estate investing" | "Own real estate for $500, zero management" |
| Passive voice | "Returns are generated by our portfolio" | "Your portfolio generates returns" |
| Jargon | "Participate in our fractionalized securities offering" | "Own a piece of a real apartment" |
| No specificity | "Low fees" | "1.5% annual management fee, no hidden charges" |
| Weak CTA | "Learn more" | "See available properties" |
| Feature focus | "Our platform uses AI-driven matching" | "We find the right property for your goals" |
